## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What are corrugated mailer boxes made of?  
Corrugated mailer boxes are made from layered paperboard with a fluted inner layer that adds strength and cushioning for product protection.

2. Are corrugated mailer boxes suitable for shipping?  
Yes, they are designed to handle shipping conditions and provide reliable protection against impact and handling.

3. Can I customize corrugated mailer boxes?
